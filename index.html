About Me

My name is Oscar Gonzalez. I was born in Arlinghton Heights, IL on June 20, 1994. When I was 3 years old I moved to Mexico for my father's work. When I was 9 years old we moved back to Illinois and have been living here ever since. I am fluent in both English and Spanish. My first language is Spanish so I have a slight accent that makes some English words very hard to pronounce.

I love to cook, play video games, watch movies, listen to music and drive my truck. I love telling jokes and making poeple laugh. After a few years in an industry that I love (cooking), I decided it was time for a change and enrolled in Northwestern University's Coding BootCamp. I am really enjoy coding and the endless possibilities of content I can create with it. I am always looking for a good challenge to test my skills.
